* **Time** – when did the action happen?
  * Past 🕗
  * Present 🕛
  * Future 🕓
* **Continuity** – is it important that the action would be happening for a certain period of time?
  * Not Continuous
  * Continuous ⏩
* **Perfection** – is it important that the action is perfected (completed)?
  * Not Perfect
  * Perfect 💯
