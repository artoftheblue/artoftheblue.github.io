**mark**

- a number or letter that is given to show the standard of somebody’s work or performance or is given to somebody for answering something correctly
    - *She got good marks for her assignment. (буквально: оценка)*

**grade**

- a mark given in an exam or for a piece of school work
    - *She got good grades in her exams. (буквально: оценка)*
- 🇺🇸 one of the levels in a school with children of similar age
    - *Sam is in (the) second grade. (буквально: группа людей, класс)*

---

**class**

- [countable + singular or plural verb] a group of students who are taught together
    - *She is the youngest in her class. (буквально: группа людей, класс)*
- [countable, uncountable] an occasion when a group of students meets to be taught
    - *I have a history class at 9 o'clock. (буквально: урок)*
- [countable] a series of classes on a particular subject
    - *She's going to evening classes in Spanish. (буквально: занятия, в принципе)*
- a group of students who finish their studies at school, college or university in a particular year
    - *the class of 2020 (буквально: выпускники)*

**form**

- 🇬🇧 a class in a school
- *Who's your form teacher? (буквально: группа людей, класс)*

**year**

- (at a school, etc.) a level that you stay in for one year; a student at a particular level
  - *She was in my year at school.*
  -  *I am a 2nd-year student.*
